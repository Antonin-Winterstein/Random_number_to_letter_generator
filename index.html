This random number generator has been designed for people who want to learn numbers of a foreign language more easily.

How to use it :

  1. Select the desired language (currently only French, English and Korean)
  2. Select the desired interval by setting a minimum and maximum value ranging from 0 to 99 (negative values do not work)
  3. Click on the "Generate" button to generate a random number between your interval, you can also press your Enter key or space bar to do that
  4. Click on the question mark to display the writing of this number in the chosen language
